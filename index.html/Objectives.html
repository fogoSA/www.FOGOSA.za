    AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

    a) Create a caring, protective and enabling environment for all those who require our services through the creation of a new Labour Desk aimed at addressing issues of unemployed people of over 35 years of age.

    b) Promote Human Rights and care for people whom are survivors of work related violence/abuse, regardless of race, colour or creed.

    c) To assist and provide professional mental health services, inclusive of material need, and any other services conducive to recovery, to all people and whom have been subjected to discrimination and prejudice based on their age at places of work.

    d) Develop growth and improved quality of Life Skills and Skills Retraining Programmes internally and in Partnership with relevant Organisations. Work in Partnership with specialised Professional Service Providers to retrain people based on their skills and experience and ensure the highest quality of care and service to enter job market.

    f) Provide services and work in collaboration with accredited Professional Departments and Agencies to ensure high quality services to job seekers considering entering the job market.

    g) Provide information and public awareness in respect of the discrimination faced by one in job related matters in work places and Human Rights and Gender-based violence/abuse to the broader community.

    h) Engage in collaborative relationships with relevant Partners for ongoing service and developmental research.

    i) Apply a humanitarian, high level of integrity and caring approach to our work and to our people.

    j) Create opportunities for Volunteers who add value to our work and respect their contributions to improve our services.

    k) Work in co-ordination and collaboratively with other relevant non-profit organisations Government Departments to expand and improve services related to Ageism.

    Goal: To promote and create a caring, protective and enabling environment for all those who are unemployed and over 35 years of age.

    Goal: Our vision is a community in which unemployment is eradicated and in which people’s dignity and self-esteem is restored, enabling them to take responsibility for their own destiny, and enjoy full access to appropriate resources.
